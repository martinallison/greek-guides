It’s the language spoken by 10-12 million people across the world, but mainly in Greece, Cyprus and parts of Albania.

There are big Greek-speaking communities in parts of the US, Germany and London too. If you can walk around central London for a day without hearing Greek at least once, we’ll buy you a gyro (γύρο).

Why would I want to learn it?

The Greek language has one of the longest histories of any language on the planet. People have been speaking some form of Greek for over three thousand years.

Its ancient form was used by the some of the first truly sophisticated philosophers, mathematicians and physicists who gave us the foundations for much of what we understand about the world today. That’s crazy.

If that doesn’t do it for you, maybe you’ve got a Greek partner (or maybe you’re looking for one...), Greek friends or Greek heritage. Perhaps Greece is your go-to place for a holiday and you finally want to be able to say thanks after ordering that souvlaki (σουβλάκι).
